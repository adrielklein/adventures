Hi, my name is Adriel. I’ve been into bikes ever since my dad taught me how to ride one.

Out of all the different bike related activities, bike touring has to be one of my favorites because…

  1. 1. It creates a deep connection with the land and it’s inhabitants
  2. 2. I meet some great people on the road, many of whom inspire me
  3. 3. It invokes a sense of adventure

I’ve done a few tours so far and each have been special and unique. In 2014 I did a cross country charity ride with Bike and Build from RI to CA. Then a couple years later I quit my job to do a self supported ride from NY to OR.

The map shows which states I have reached on my bike so far. Some day, I hope to reach them all.
